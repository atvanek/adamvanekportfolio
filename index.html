Hi, I'm Adam!

I'm a Front End Web Developer who loves to create efficient and engaging web experiences.

Ever since my dad brought home our first Power Macintosh in the late 90's, I've been teaching myself everything I can about computers and navigating the web. For the past decade, I've had the pleasure of working as a professional musical theater actor. My shift into web design was rooted in a desire to connect people via an online presence in and age where connection can be hard to come by. My background in theater gives me a unique persepctive on human experience and emotion, which I strive to translate into personal, efficient, and engaging web experiences.
